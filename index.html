FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I know if my roof needs to be replaced?

If your roof has missing shingles, recurring leaks, visible sagging, storm damage, or is near the end of its expected lifespan, it may be time to consider replacement. A professional inspection will give you a clear answer.

What does a roof replacement estimate include?

A proper estimate should outline materials, labor, tear-off, cleanup, ventilation needs, warranty details, and any recommended repairs.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements are completed in one to a few days, depending on roof size, weather, materials, and project complexity.

Do you help with insurance claims?

Yes. We can inspect storm damage, document visible issues, and help you understand the roofing portion of your insurance process.

What types of roofing materials do you offer?

We offer quality roofing systems selected for durability, curb appeal, and dependable protection in the Tri-State climate.
